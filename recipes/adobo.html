Adobo

Discription

Adobo is a quintessential Filipino dish known for its rich, savory flavors and tender meat. Traditionally made with pork or chicken, it's marinated in a mixture of vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and black peppercorns before being slowly simmered to perfection.

The vinegar not only adds a tangy kick but also helps tenderize the meat, creating a melt-in-your-mouth texture. Adobo is often enjoyed with steamed rice, and its versatile nature allows for variations across regions and households, making it a beloved staple in Filipino cuisine.<

  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat.
  2. Sear the meat on oil pan.
  3. Pour the marinade sauce and let simmer.
  4. Adjust seasoning on your own taste.
  5. Serve.
