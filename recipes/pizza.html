Pizza

Discription

Pizza, a beloved culinary classic, is a round, flatbread topped with savory ingredients and baked to perfection. Its signature combination of gooey melted cheese, tangy tomato sauce, and a variety of toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives, makes it a universally adored dish.

Whether enjoyed as a quick bite on the go, shared among friends during gatherings, or savored as a comforting meal at home, pizza's versatility and irresistible taste make it a timeless favorite around the globe.

List of Ingridients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • pepperoni and mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the pizza dough.
  2. Roll out the dough into a round shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Sprinkle toppings and cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Bake and serve.
